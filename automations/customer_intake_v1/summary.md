# Customer Intake & Qualification — Client-Facing Summary

## What This Automation Does

When a new potential client fills out your intake form, this automation instantly reviews their submission and tells you whether they're worth pursuing — and how to follow up.

## How It Works (Plain Language)

1. **A lead submits your intake form** with basic details about their business, their challenges, and their budget.
2. **The system reviews the submission automatically** using AI to assess fit based on budget, timeline, clarity of need, and industry alignment.
3. **Each lead gets a score and a recommended action:**
   - **Hot lead** → you get notified immediately to schedule a call.
   - **Warm lead** → an information packet is sent automatically.
   - **Cold lead** → they're added to a follow-up nurture sequence.
   - **Not a fit** → no action is taken, and the record is logged.
4. **Everything is recorded** so you can review every lead, every score, and every decision at any time.

## What You Don't Have to Do Manually
- Read and evaluate every form submission.
- Decide who to follow up with first.
- Send initial responses.

## What You Stay in Control Of
- The intake form questions.
- The follow-up templates and messaging.
- Any lead marked for manual review (you'll be notified and can override any decision).

## What Gets Logged
Every submission — whether it qualifies or not — is stored with the AI's reasoning, the score, and the recommended action. Nothing is lost.
